# Roles and Permissions

Coptogram uses a robust Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system to ensure that every participant has the exact permissions they need to fulfill their mission.

<Callout type="info">
  The system supports multi-tenant isolation, allowing large organizations like Dioceses to manage separate sub-ministries under one umbrella.
</Callout>

## The Organizational Architecture

The **Organization Role** is designed for centralized management. When an entity is registered as an Organization, it gains the power to:
1. **Delegated Administration**: Assign managers to specific modules.
2. **Branding Control**: Enforce identity across all sub-portals.
3. **Advanced Reporting**: Access aggregated analytics for all affiliated students.

<div className="alert success">
  **Organization accounts** can also benefit from B2B pricing models and bulk enrollment tools.
</div>
