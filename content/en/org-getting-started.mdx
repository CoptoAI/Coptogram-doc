    description: |
      Step-by-step guide to configuring your organizational profile and branding. Follow these steps to ensure your ministry's identity is consistent across the platform.

      <ol class="steps">
        <li>**Navigate to Admin Dashboard**: Log in to your Coptogram account and select your Organization from the top switcher. Click on **Settings** in the left sidebar.</li>
        <li>**Logo Upload**: Under the **Branding** tab, find the 'Logo' section. Click 'Upload' and select a high-resolution PNG or SVG (recommended 400x400px).
        
        ![Dashboard Branding Interface - Logo Upload Section](https://picsum.photos/seed/setup-logo/800/450)
        </li>
        <li>**Configure Brand Colors**: Select your **Primary Color** using the hex code or color picker. This color will be used for buttons, icons, and highlights in your students' apps.
        
        ![Color Configuration Panel - Primary and Secondary selection](https://picsum.photos/seed/setup-colors/800/450)
        </li>
        <li>**Save & Preview**: Scroll to the bottom and click **Save Changes**. You can immediately preview how this looks by clicking the 'Switch to Student View' button at the top right.</li>
      </ol>

      <div class="alert info">**Tip:** Use a transparent background for your logo to ensure it looks professional on both light and dark themes.</div>
